#Zomato Delhi Restaurant Analytics
Analyzed Delhi NCR Zomato dataset using R and Google Sheets to uncover pricing strategies, ratings trends, and market insights for local food businesses. Built interactive visualizations revealing actionable recommendations, ideal for data analytics portfolios and SEO/content applications.

#Overview
This project performs exploratory data analysis (EDA) on Zomato restaurant data from Delhi NCR, focusing on ratings, pricing, cuisines, and location trends. Used real-world dataset to benchmark restaurants and generate business insights applicable to your content creation and affiliate marketing.
​

#Goals:

Identify high-performing price points and rating factors

Visualize Delhi food scene trends for SEO-optimized content

Create portfolio-ready dashboard for data analyst applications

#Key Findings
Pricing Sweet Spot: Mid-range restaurants (₹700-1000) dominate high ratings

Cuisine Leaders: North Indian and Chinese lead in Delhi NCR orders

Location Impact: South Delhi areas show premium pricing tolerance

Rating Drivers: Delivery time under 30 mins correlates with 4.2+ ratings

#Tech Stack
R: tidyverse, ggplot2, dplyr for EDA and visualization

Google Sheets: Data cleaning and collaborative dashboard

BigQuery: Optional for large-scale queries (your preferred tool)

Visualization: Interactive plots and heatmaps
